Dotto

A minimal multilingual data-related product!




A Dotto covers an specific data-science concept efficiently in different programming language. Let's be right to the point and share our knowledge effective!

Dottos are created from Dots covering a sub-section of a Dotto. Each Dot divided to three parts: Instruction, Codes, and Results, which are answering to a question efficiently.

Dottos can team up and make a line. Lines would be changed dynamically based on the new Dottos come into place. A Dotto can live in multiple lines.
